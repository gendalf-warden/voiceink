Native macOS menu-bar voice dictation. Local Whisper + LLM. No cloud, no subscription.

v0.5.013 · 14 MB · macOS 13+ · Apple Silicon

What it does

  • Hold Fn anywhere, dictate, release — text is pasted at the cursor
  • Whisper large-v3-turbo for transcription (runs locally on your Mac)
  • Qwen 2.5 for punctuation & light editing (also local)
  • Transcribe audio/video files from the menu bar
  • Custom replacements dictionary for tricky names

Auto-update

After install, use the menu-bar item «Check for Updates…» to get new releases.
